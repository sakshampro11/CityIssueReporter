How Points And Badges Work

Points

  • +5 for reporting a new issue
  • +2 for verifying someone else's issue
  • +10 bonus when one of your reported issues gets resolved

Badges

  • First Report — report 1 issue
  • Community Watcher — verify 5 issues
  • Problem Solver — have 1 of your issues resolved
