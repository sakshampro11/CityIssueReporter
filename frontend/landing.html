Our Mission

About Lokally

Lokally is a citizen-driven platform designed to make city living better.

We believe that a community is strongest when its members are empowered to speak up about the issues that affect their daily lives.

From potholes to broken streetlights, Lokally makes it simple to report problems, track progress, and work together with local authorities to build a better, safer, and cleaner neighbourhood.

Quick Guide

How It Works

1

Sign Up

Create an account using your basic details. Your logged-in dashboard lets you save reports, track current updates, and prefill details automatically.

2

Report an Issue

Click the 'New Issue' button, write a short description, upload a photo, and let our integrated AI categorise and summarise the issue automatically.

3

Track Progress

Watch as issues transition from Pending to Resolved on your interactive feed. Get local issues solved in collaboration with authorities.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We only collect details that are essential to process your reports and keep you informed.

✓

Minimal Collection

We collect your email and name solely for updates, security, and dashboard verification.

✓

No Data Selling

Your personal details are never sold to external advertising networks or shared with third parties.

✓

Public Transparency

Report summaries and photos are shared with your community, but your email and details remain private.
